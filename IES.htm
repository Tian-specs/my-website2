Toy Car Assemblly

The Toy Car Assembly activity was one of the major highlights during the Engineering Days, particularly under the Industrial Engineering Departmental Events. This competition engaged students from first year to fourth year in a dynamic and highly competitive challenge designed to simulate a real-world assembly line environment.


Research Defense

The Research Defense serves as a formal academic evaluation where Industrial Engineering students present and justify their research proposals or studies before a panel of faculty experts. This academic milestone involves a structured presentation of the research title, problem statement, theoretical framework, methodology, and initial findings, followed by a rigorous question-and-answer segment where students must defend the validity, feasibility, and relevance of their study.

Company Visit

The Company Tour provided an immersive, educational experience at Profoods International Corporation, one of the leading manufacturers of processed tropical fruit products in the Philippines. During the visit, students observed the complete production lifecycle—from the initial receiving and cleaning of raw fruits, to extraction, quality control, dehydration or concentration, and finally, packaging and labeling of finished goods.


Industrial Engineering Research Congress

The Regional Industrial Engineering Research Congress is a prestigious academic event that brings together Industrial Engineering students and faculty from across the region to share innovative research outputs and evidence-based solutions to real-world industrial challenges.
